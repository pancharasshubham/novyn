# NOVYN Experience

## Core Question

What keeps attracting my attention?

NOVYN helps users understand what repeatedly captures their attention.

Search is a tool.

Reflection is the product.

---

## User Journey

Import
↓
Recognition
↓
Reflection
↓
Rediscovery
↓
Search

---

## Homepage Goals

The homepage should answer:

1. What keeps attracting me?
2. Who influences me?
3. What should I revisit?
4. What can I search?

---

## Information Hierarchy

Primary:
- Reflection
- Attention
- Recurring interests

Secondary:
- Influences
- Recent saves
- Rediscovery

Utility:
- Search
- Import

---

## Search

Search remains highly visible.

Search is a utility.

Search is not the homepage.

---

## Reflection

NOVYN observes:

- repetition
- consistency
- recurring attention

NOVYN does not:

- diagnose
- predict
- judge

---

## Influences

People shape attention.

Repeated creators are evidence.

---

## Rediscovery

The goal is:

"I forgot I saved this."

---

## Layout Principles

Desktop-first.

Use the available screen width.

Avoid narrow content columns.

Avoid excessive scrolling.

Avoid horizontal carousels.

Use cards only when they improve understanding.

---

## Avoid

- dashboards
- KPI cards
- productivity software
- analytics interfaces
- infinite feeds
- social timelines
