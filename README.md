# NOVYN

> What keeps attracting your attention?

NOVYN is a personal saved-content reflection tool.

It helps you search, revisit, and understand the content you repeatedly save.

The first version is built entirely around Instagram saved posts.

Instead of asking:

> What should I do?

NOVYN asks:

> What do I keep returning to?

---

## Why NOVYN?

Most saved content eventually disappears.

Thousands of reels, posts, tutorials, ideas, and inspirations become digital clutter.

People save things because they:

- want to learn something
- admire someone
- aspire to become something
- intend to build something
- don't want to forget

NOVYN helps surface those recurring signals.

---

## Core Questions

- What have I been repeatedly saving?
- Which creators influence my attention?
- What interests have remained consistent?
- What content have I forgotten?
- What keeps returning to my attention?

---

## Features

### Instagram Saved Posts Import

Import your exported Instagram saved posts JSON.

- 4,000+ saves supported
- Entirely local processing
- No server upload required

---

### Universal Search

Search across:

- captions
- creators
- usernames
- hashtags
- URLs

Instant, client-side search.

---

### Attention Patterns

NOVYN identifies recurring attention.

Examples:

- topics that repeatedly appear
- interests that remain consistent
- creators that influence attention

---

### Influences

See which creators repeatedly appear in your saved content.

Attention is often shaped by people.

NOVYN makes those influences visible.

---

### Rediscovery

Resurface forgotten content.

Because saving something once does not mean remembering it.

---

## Philosophy

NOVYN does not tell users who they should become.

NOVYN only reflects:

- repetition
- consistency
- recurring attention

Suggestions must always be supported by evidence.

Observation → Evidence → Reflection

The user decides what matters.

---
